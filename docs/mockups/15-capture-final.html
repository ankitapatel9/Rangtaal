Complete flow:

  1. FAB tap on session detail → full-screen camera opens
  2. Photo mode: tap shutter → captures → compresses → uploads instantly → returns to session
  3. Video mode: toggle to VIDEO, hold shutter to record (or tap to start/stop) → compresses on-device → uploads → returns
  4. Upload happens in background — gallery shows spinner on the new tile, plum toast confirms "Photo/Video added"
  5. "+ Add from roll" in gallery header → opens camera roll picker (photos + videos)
  6. Compression: photos resized to 1080px max dimension, videos to 720p. Keeps uploads fast and storage costs low.
  7. No duration cap for now — but videos over 5 min show a warning "Large video, upload may take a moment"
