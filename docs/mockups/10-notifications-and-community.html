Key UX details:

  • Bell icon — visible on all screens in the header. Gold badge with unread count. Tapping opens the notification list screen.
  • Push → deep link — tapping a push notification (from lock screen or notification center) opens the app directly to the relevant screen.
  • In-app tap — tapping a notification in the list navigates to the same route. Marks as read.
  • Community — admin taps a user card to toggle payment (bottom sheet confirmation, not Alert). Search filters by name. "Paid" pill in gold, "Unpaid" pill in gray.
