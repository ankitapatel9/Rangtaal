My recommendation: Lucide (C)

Lucide is the quietest — thin strokes let the content and brand colors do the talking. With the Plum & Gold palette, heavier icons (SF/Phosphor) can feel busy. Lucide is also the lightest dependency (lucide-react-native, ~20kb). Active tab gets a thicker stroke in gold, inactive stays thin in gray.
