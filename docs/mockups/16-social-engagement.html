Engagement system:

  • Likes — heart icon, tap to toggle. Filled gold when liked. Count next to icon.
  • Comments — threaded. Avatar + name + text + timestamp. "Reply" link in gold opens reply input.
  • Replies — indented under parent comment. Smaller avatars. Can go 1 level deep (no nested replies on replies).
  • Like on comments — small ♡ with count on individual comments.
  • Gallery photos — dark background (full-screen view), engagement bar below photo.
  • Tutorial videos — warm ivory background, engagement below video player.
  • Same data model for both — a `comments` collection with `parentId` (media or tutorial) and `parentType` field.
