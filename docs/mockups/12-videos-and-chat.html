Design notes:

  • Videos — grouped by session date, large video thumbnails with play overlay. Lock badge on unpaid. Paywall banner at top for unpaid users. Paid users see no banner.
  • Chat — iMessage-style bubbles. Own messages: plum bg, white text, right-aligned. Others: white bg, plum text, left-aligned with avatar. Gold send button. Date dividers. Single community channel for MVP (no threads, no DMs).
  • Chat is a future build — this mockup establishes the visual direction. The actual chat feature (Firestore real-time messages collection, typing indicators, read receipts) is its own implementation phase.
