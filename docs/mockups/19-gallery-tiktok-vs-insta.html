B: TikTok / Reels

Full-screen vertical scroll. One post per screen, swipe up for next. Right-side action buttons. Best for video-first content. Immersive but photos feel awkward at full-screen.

My take:

Rangtaal has a mix of photos AND videos. Instagram (A) handles both naturally — photos fill the width, videos play inline, multi-photo carousels work. TikTok (B) is great for video-only feeds but photos stretched to full-screen look odd. You could also do a hybrid: Instagram feed by default, tap a video to go full-screen TikTok-style playback.
